<h2><strong>Auto Unshift & Dismount for Classic, SoD + TBC</strong></h2>
<p><em>"Action Blocked"? Not anymore.</em></p>

<p><strong>FlightReady</strong> is the ultimate fix for Druids, Shamans, and mounted players in World of Warcraft: Classic Era, SoD, and Anniversary. It ensures you can talk to a Flight Master and fly away instantly, without manually cancelling your form or mount first.</p>

<hr>

<h2>ğŸŒŸ Why FlightReady?</h2>

<p>Blizzard's "Classic" client strictly protects shapeshift cancellation actions. Most other addons try to solve this with scripts that cause "Action Blocked" errors or UI taint, forcing you to reload.</p>

<p><strong>FlightReady is different.</strong> It uses invisible <strong>Secure Macro Overlays (SMO)</strong> technology. By placing a secure button over the flight path, it executes the cancellation as a hardware-level command.</p>

<ul>
    <li><strong>100% Secure</strong>: No taint, no errors.</li>
    <li><strong>100% Reliable</strong>: Works every single time.</li>
    <li><strong>One Click</strong>: Click the flight path -> Form cancels -> You fly.</li>
</ul>
